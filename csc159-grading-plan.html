Course Description: This course will help students learn how to perform an effective penetration test in an enterprise network environment that must be attacked, exploited, evaded, and defended. This course also covers how to pen test IoT systems, OT systems, how to write your own exploits, build your own tools, conduct advanced binaries exploitation, double pivot to access hidden networks, and also customize scripts/exploits to get into the innermost segments of the network.

Late Work Policy

  • Labs: 10% deduction per day late (maximum 3 days)
  • Assignments: 10% deduction per day late (maximum 5 days)
  • Quizzes: Must be taken during scheduled time (no make-ups without prior arrangement)
  • Final Project: No late submissions accepted

Academic Integrity

  • All work must be original
  • Collaboration on labs is encouraged but each student must submit their own work
  • Tools and exploits used must be documented and cited
  • Plagiarism will result in a zero for the assignment and possible course failure

Attendance and Participation

  • Attendance is critical due to hands-on nature of course
  • More than 3 unexcused absences may result in course failure
  • Lab work cannot be completed outside of scheduled lab time without prior approval

Required Materials

  • Textbook: EC-Council Certified Penetration Testing Professional Complete Series
  • Access to virtual lab environment (provided by college)
  • Personal laptop recommended for homework assignments
